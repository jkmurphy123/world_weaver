# World Bible - New Meridian

- Genre: Cyberpunk corporate dystopia
- Tone: Investigative and street-level with high-stakes institutional conflict
- Premise: In 2074, New Meridian is a corporate city-state in the Strait of Malacca where five megacorporations govern without democracy, social class is defined by vertical geography, and emergent AIs and underground networks destabilize corporate control.

## Organizations
- Meridian Council: Consortium through which the five megacorporations govern New Meridian.
- Drift Council: Community-level representation body for Drift residents.
- Circuit Monks: Decentralized node operators distributing sensitive documents.
- Red Lotus Syndicate: Underbelly gang actor referenced in baseline crime archetypes, recently implicated by Meridian Council in high-grade digital credential forgeries escalating gang competition in The Underbelly.
- Null Protocol: Technically advanced Free Circuit cell linked to Blackout Hour in 2073.
- GhostLine: Mercenary identity-forgery and credential spoofing service.
- The Bleeding Edge: Political augmentation-jailbreak network running risky mod clinics in The Drift. Currently under sweeping enforcement crackdown by Meridian Council due to spike in mod-related injuries and rising political profile.

## Locations
- New Meridian Special Administrative Zone: Artificial archipelago in the Strait of Malacca, around 45 square km.
- Meridian Central: Corporate core district containing ZephyrSpire and Vault Tower.
- The Crown: Upper-tier district containing Tower Axiom and Luminos Sanctuary.
- The Spine: Heavy industrial corridor and Harkon manufacturing stronghold.
- The Stack: Dense lower-tier area and organizing base for labor and AI-rights activism.
- The Underbelly: Informal free-net and black-market network zone under corporate oversight gaps.
- The Drift: Platform settlements for unregistered residents and precarious labor populations.
- Platform K-7: Known base area for Bleeding Edge augmentation clinics in The Drift.
